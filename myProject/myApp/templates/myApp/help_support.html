Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Appointment" card on your dashboard or using the Find Doctor feature to select a specific doctor. Choose your preferred date and time, and confirm your booking.
How can I cancel my appointment?
To cancel an appointment, go to your dashboard and find the appointment in the "Upcoming Appointments" section. Click the "Cancel" button and confirm your decision. Please note that some cancellations may be subject to our cancellation policy.
How do I update my profile information?
You can update your profile information by clicking on "Profile & Settings" in the side menu. Here you can edit your personal information, medical history, and emergency contacts.
What should I do if I need emergency medical care?
For medical emergencies, please call emergency services (911) immediately. This platform is not intended for emergency medical situations and should not be used in life-threatening circumstances.
